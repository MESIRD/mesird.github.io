MoneyLib

This is an iOS application that allows you to record daily expense and income. It’s much handy that you can make a record in seconds after every payment. With simplest UI and easiest interaction, you’ll love using this tiny app!

Features
  • Fast accounting Making a record in seconds, simple interaction and easy to use.
  • Smart chart Powerful and smart chart allows you to analyse data in various dimensions.
  • Various categories A mass of categories with customized content.
  • iCloud synchronization Synchronizing accounting data between all your apple devices, keep everything up to date.
  • Fingerprint / Passcode validation Protecting your data from being watching by others, security is important.
  • Multi-pocket management More than one pocket you can record expense and income data.
